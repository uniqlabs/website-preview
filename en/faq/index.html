What methods is UNIQ based on?

UNIQ is based on long-proven methods as well as the latest findings from cognitive psychology. Among others, we are working with the A-B-C model by Albert Ellis to resolve so-called self-limiting beliefs during a coaching call.

Which goals can I achieve with UNIQ?

You can tackle goals both big (within several years) and small (within a few weeks). The most important thing is that your goals have a measurable outcome and are realistic, i.e. they are - in principle - achievable for you. If you want to achieve a big goal we help you break it down to your first intermediate goal and achieve that.
