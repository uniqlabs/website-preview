Founders

Anke Kaupp

Co-Founder, Chief Psychologist

Anke has 25 years of experience as a clinical therapist, business and life coach and has founded two psychological institutes in the Stuttgart area. She has empowered thousands of people to unfold their true potential whatever their circumstances.

Anke specialized in Rational Emotive and Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (REBT) with Professor Albert Ellis in New York and is also a facilitator of neuro-feedback. She is a nationally renowned ADHD expert and a sought-after keynote speaker on the topic, has counseled children and parents and trained thousands of therapists, social workers and teachers in dealing with ADHD.

Anke leads the development of the UNIQ coaching method and oversees the training of our coaches and supervisors.

Insa Klasing

Co-Founder, Chief Executive Officer

Insa has a passion for growing businesses and people, most recently as GM of KFC Germany, where she doubled the business. Before that Insa built the German business of innocent smoothies from scratch. Prior to that she was Strategy Consultant in Bain & Company´s London office. Insa began her career with Action Aid in New Delhi, where she published a book on disability in India. She is founder of Zindagi, an NGO supporting basic education for the poor in the Himalaya.

Insa holds a BA in Politics, Philosophy and Economics (PPE) from the University of Oxford and an MA in South Asian Area Studies from the University of London. She is on the board of AmCham Germany.

At UNIQ Insa is responsible for the growth strategy and business development.

Klaas Klasing

Co-Founder, Chief Technology Officer

Klaas fell in love with software development when he wrote his first lines of BASIC at the age of 7. He holds an M.Sc. from the University of Michigan, where he developed a deeper interest in algorithms and artificial intelligence. During his Ph.D. at TU Munich he taught a class on machine learning and developed an autonomous robot that successfully found its way to Munich’s central square in the end.

His passion for product development brought him to BMW research, where he was in charge of prototyping innovative apps and services in the area of door-to-door mobility. In 2015 he left BMW to found his first startup LOCLET, a location-based trivia game that lets you explore cities playfully.

At UNIQ Klaas is responsible for the digitalization of the coaching experience.
