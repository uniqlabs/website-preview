Anke has 25 years of experience as clinical therapist, business and life coach and founded two psychological institutes. She has empowered thousands of people to unfold their true potential whatever their circumstances.

Anke specialized in Rational Emotive and Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (REBT) with Professor Albert Ellis in New York and is also a facilitator of neuro-feedback. She is a nationally renowned ADHD expert and a sought-after keynote speaker, counseling children and parents as well as training thousands of therapists, social workers and teachers in ADHD.

Anke leads the development of the UNIQ coaching method and trains our coaches and supervisors.
